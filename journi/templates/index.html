Welcome to Journi Jr.

Your Companion for Family Health and Wellness on the Go

What is Journi Jr.?

Journi Jr. is a mobile app designed to help families navigate healthcare challenges while traveling.

Mission

To empower families by providing accessible, reliable health information and services.

Vision

To be the leading mobile health companion for traveling families.

Key Features:

Emergency Health Locator

Easily locate nearby pediatric specialists or emergency health services.

Travel Kits & Tips

Personalized travel health guides for your little ones.

FAQs

Are the health alerts up-to-date?
Yes, our health alerts are regularly updated to ensure the latest information on local diseases, outbreaks, or health advisories is available.
Is the app free to use?
The basic version of the app is free to use, which includes access to core features such as the symptom checker and health alerts.
How do I contact support if I have questions?
You can contact our support team through multiple channels inside the app or via email.
Can I access the app offline?
While some features require an internet connection, you can access certain features offline.
How do I update my profile information?
Navigate to the Profile section within the app to edit your details.

Our Story

At Journi Jr., our journey began during the Da Vinci Competition, where we recognized the urgent need for a reliable travel companion designed specifically for families.
